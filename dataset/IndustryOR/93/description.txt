A company blends two types of crude oil (A and B) to produce two types of gasoline (Type I and Type II). The minimum proportion of crude oil A in gasoline Types I and II is 50% and 60%, respectively. The selling prices are 4800 yuan/t and 5600 yuan/t, respectively. The company has current inventories of 500 t of crude oil A and 1000 t of crude oil B, and they can purchase up to 1500 t of crude oil A from the market. The market price for crude oil A is: 10,000 yuan/t for purchases up to 500 t; 8,000 yuan/t for the portion exceeding 500 t but not exceeding 1000 t; 6,000 yuan/t for the portion exceeding 1000 t. How should the company plan its purchasing and processing of crude oil?
