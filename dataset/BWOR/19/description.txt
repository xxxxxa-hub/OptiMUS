
    \noindent\textbf{5.12 Truck Delivery Problem (Set Covering Problem)} Longyun Company must currently deliver goods to 5 customers: 1 unit of goods to Customer A, 2 units to Customer B, 3 units to Customer C, 4 units to Customer D, 8 units to Customer E. The company has 4 trucks, each used only once: Truck 1: capacity of 2 units, Truck 2: capacity of 6 units, Truck 3: capacity of 8 units, Truck 4: capacity of 11 units. The cost of using truck j is c_j. Constraints: A truck cannot deliver to both Customer A and Customer C. A truck cannot deliver to both Customer B and Customer D. Objective:Formulate an integer programming model to determine how to assign the deliveries to the trucks to minimize the total transportation cost.
