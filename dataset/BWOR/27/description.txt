
  A hospital operates 24 hours a day, and the number of nurses needed in each time period is as follows: $2\!:\!00\!\sim\!6\!:\!00\!\longrightarrow\!10$ nurses, $6\!:\!00\!\sim$ 10:00\!\longrightarrow\!15$ nurses, $10:00\!\sim\!14:00\!\longrightarrow\!25$ nurses, $14:00\!\sim\!18\!:\!00\!\longrightarrow\!20$ nurses, $18\,;00\!\sim\!22\!:\!00\!\longrightarrow\!18$ nurses,$22\!:\!00\!\sim\!2\!:\!00\!-\!\!\!-\!12$ nurses. Nurses are scheduled in 6 shifts, starting at: $2:00\,,6:00\,,10:00\,,14:00\,,18:00\,,22:00$ and each nurse works 8 consecutive hours.

Task:Determine the minimum number of nurses the hospital needs to schedule in total in order to meet the staffing requirements.
